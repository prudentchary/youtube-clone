TERMS AND CONDITIONS FOR COMPLIANCE BY PROSPECTIVE CORPS MEMBERS

* I agree to abide by the rules governing the Scheme.

* I agree to serve for 12 unbroken calender months.

* I agree to wear the approved uniform for prescribed activity.

* I agree not to modify my uniform.

* I agree not to undertake night journey throughout the duration of my National service.

* I agree abide by the Bye-Laws for Corps Members.

* I agree that all the information supplied by me is correct.

* I agree that where it is discovered that I misinformed the Scheme, necessary disciplinary action should be taken against me.
